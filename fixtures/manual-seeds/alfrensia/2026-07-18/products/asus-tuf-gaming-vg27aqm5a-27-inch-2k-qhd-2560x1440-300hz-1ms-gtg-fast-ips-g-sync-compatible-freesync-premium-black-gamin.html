FAQ

What is the advantage of Fast IPS technology?

ASUS Fast IPS technology allows the display’s liquid crystal elements to switch up to 4x faster than conventional IPS panels. This results in a lightning-fast 0.3ms minimum GTG response time, virtually eliminating motion blur and providing a competitive advantage in fast-paced titles.

How does DisplayWidget Center improve the user experience?

DisplayWidget Center is a utility that allows you to adjust monitor settings via a mouse rather than using the physical OSD buttons. It provides an intuitive interface to tweak AI Visual settings, color temperatures, and GamePlus features instantly.

Is the monitor compatible with both NVIDIA and AMD GPUs?

Yes. The VG27AQM5A supports both NVIDIA G-Sync Compatible and AMD FreeSync Premium technologies. This ensures a tear-free, low-latency gaming experience regardless of which graphics card architecture you choose for your rig.
