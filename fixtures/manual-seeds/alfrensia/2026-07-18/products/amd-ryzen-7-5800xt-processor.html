AMD Ryzen 7 5800XT Processor:

The AMD Ryzen 7 5800XT Processor delivers proven, exceptional game performance for enthusiast desktops. This **AMD Ryzen 7 5800XT Processor** features 8 CPU Cores and 16 Threads, providing robust multitasking and powerful processing for demanding applications. Built on the TSMC 7nm FinFET process technology, it offers efficient power consumption. Furthermore, it is unlocked for overclocking, allowing enthusiasts to push performance even further.

AMD Ryzen 7 5800XT Processor: Elite Game Performance & Overclocking:

This AMD Ryzen 7 5800XT Processor operates with a Base Clock of 3.8 GHz. It can reach a Max. Boost Clock of up to 4.8 GHz. Its 8 CPU Cores and 16 Threads efficiently handle intensive workloads. Specifically, the processor includes a substantial 32 MB of L3 Cache and 4 MB of L2 Cache. This significantly contributes to its responsive performance in gaming, content creation, and productivity tasks.

AMD Ryzen 7 5800XT Processor: Advanced Architecture & Connectivity:

The AMD Ryzen 7 5800XT Processor is based on the Zen 3 Processor Architecture. This provides significant performance improvements over previous generations. It utilizes the AM4 CPU Socket, ensuring broad compatibility with a wide range of motherboards, including X570, B550, A520, X470, and B450 chipsets. It supports PCIe® 4.0 for high-bandwidth graphics cards and other expansion devices. For system memory, it supports DDR4 up to 3200 MT/s in a dual-channel configuration, providing ample bandwidth for system operations.

AMD Ryzen 7 5800XT Processor: Thermal Solution & Key Technologies:

This **AMD Ryzen 7 5800XT Processor** comes with an AMD Wraith Prism Thermal Solution (PIB), providing effective cooling and vibrant RGB lighting right out of the box. It has a Default TDP of 105W and a Max. Operating Temperature (Tjmax) of 90°C. Supported technologies include AMD StoreMI Technology, AMD SenseMI Technology, AMD Ryzen™ Master Utility for advanced control, and AMD Ryzen™ VR-Ready Premium. These enhance its capabilities for various workloads and virtual reality experiences.

Explore compatible AMD Processors or check AMD Products for other options. Find full details on the official AMD Ryzen 7 5800XT product page.
