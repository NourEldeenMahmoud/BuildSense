Philips 24E2G2200 144Hz Gaming Monitor

The Philips 24E2G2200 gaming monitor is a fantastic choice for fluid, responsive gameplay. This 23.8-inch display features a rapid 144Hz refresh rate and a blazingly fast 0.5ms Motion Picture Response Time (MPRT), ensuring you stay ahead in any fast-paced game. Paired with a vibrant IPS panel and Adaptive Sync, it delivers stunning visuals with no tearing or stuttering.

Key Features at a Glance:

Display: 23.8″ FHD IPS

Refresh Rate: 144Hz

Response Time: 0.5ms MPRT

Adaptive Sync: Yes

HDR: HDR 10 Supported

Smooth Gameplay, Stunning Visuals:

This monitor’s 144Hz refresh rate and 0.5ms MPRT deliver fluid, blur-free images, giving you a serious advantage in competitive gaming. The IPS panel provides vibrant colors and wide 178° viewing angles, ensuring the picture looks great from any position. With Adaptive Sync support, screen tearing is a thing of the past, as it synchronizes the monitor’s refresh rate with your GPU’s output for a perfectly smooth experience.

Designed for Comfort and Performance:

Beyond its gaming prowess, this monitor supports HDR 10, offering a wider range of brightness and contrast for a more immersive visual experience. Features like LowBlue Mode and Flicker-free technology are included to reduce eye fatigue during long gaming sessions. The display is also coated with an anti-glare finish to minimize reflections, letting you focus on the action. Find more Philips products or explore our full selection of gaming monitors.

💡 Pro Tip:

To achieve the full 144Hz refresh rate, be sure to connect the monitor using the DisplayPort 1.4 cable, as older standards may not support the maximum refresh rate at FHD resolution.
