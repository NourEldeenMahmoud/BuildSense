Lian Li O11D Dynamic Mini V2 Flow White Mid Tower Gaming Case

The Lian Li O11D Dynamic Mini V2 Flow redefines the compact showcase chassis, delivering a stunning 45.38L pillar-less dual tempered glass design paired with a revolutionary 10° slanted GPU base. Available at Alfrensia for 4,699 EGP, this white edition comes pre-equipped with five reverse-blade PWM fans, creating an optimized intake wind tunnel that supports massive 360mm radiators and next-gen Back-Connect motherboards for enthusiasts in Cairo and Alexandria.

⚡ Engineering Highlights at a Glance:

  • Pre-Tuned Airflow: 5x 120mm reverse-blade PWM fans included (3 bottom, 2 side intake).
  • Thermal Innovation: 10° slanted bottom base channels cool air directly to the GPU.
  • Maximum Compatibility: Supports ATX, mATX, ITX, and ATX Back-Connect motherboards.
  • Massive Cooling Support: Up to 360mm top radiator, 240mm side radiator, and 9 total fan mounts.
  • Modular Flexibility: Movable mATX standoffs, removable top bracket, and dual-purpose drive cages.

Who is the O11D Dynamic Mini V2 Flow For?

🎨 Premium Showcase BuildersEnthusiasts who demand the iconic O11 pillar-less glass aesthetic. The 10° slanted base naturally angles your GPU toward the glass, making it the undeniable centerpiece of your build.
🌪️ Thermal Performance SeekersGamers running hot RTX 4080/4090 or RX 7900 cards. The pre-installed reverse fans and slanted base achieve up to 6% better GPU cooling performance compared to standard layouts.
🔌 Next-Gen Back-Connect AdoptersEarly adopters of ASUS BTF or MSI Project Zero motherboards. The case features dedicated routing channels and a built-in 24-pin cable bracket to hide all wiring behind the tray seamlessly.

💡 Expert Advice & Build Considerations

📐 ATX PSU Socket Clearance Guidance

The extended side mesh elegantly accommodates ATX PSUs up to 200mm. However, to ensure the side panel closes flush and the mesh functions optimally, your PSU’s power socket must be recessed at least 12mm from the edge of the PSU housing.

⚠️ Back-Connect PCIe Slot Nuance

The O11D Mini V2 features a compact 5-slot expansion layout. If your specific ATX Back-Connect motherboard has an M.2 slot located directly under the CPU, it will align with PCIe slot 2. In this scenario, you will need to utilize the included vertical GPU mount along with a 200mm riser cable (sold separately) for proper installation. All mATX and ITX boards remain 100% plug-and-play compatible.

🌡️ Extreme Multi-Slot GPU Clearance

If you are installing a massive GPU thicker than 72mm into the second PCIe slot, the card’s shroud may physically obstruct the bottom intake fans. For extreme multi-slot cards, we highly recommend mounting in the primary top slot or utilizing the adjustable vertical GPU bracket to maintain optimal thermal performance.

Expert Recommendation: The Ultimate Wind Tunnel Setup

To maximize the Flow Edition’s thermal advantage, utilize the 3 bottom and 2 side reverse-blade fans as pure intakes, pulling cool air directly through the 10° slanted base onto your GPU. Mount up to three 120mm fans on the removable top bracket as exhaust. This creates a perfect positive pressure environment that not only keeps your RTX 4080/4090 ice-cold but also forces dust out of unfiltered gaps, keeping your white aesthetic pristine for years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the O11D Dynamic Mini V2 Flow include fans?

Yes, the Flow edition comes pre-installed with five 120mm reverse-blade PWM fans (three at the bottom, two on the side) to create an optimized intake wind tunnel right out of the box.

Does this case support Back-Connect motherboards?

Yes, it fully supports ATX Back-Connect motherboards for a zero-cable front aesthetic. Note that due to the compact 5-slot design, if your specific board has an M.2 slot directly under the CPU, you may need to use the included vertical GPU mount with a riser cable.
