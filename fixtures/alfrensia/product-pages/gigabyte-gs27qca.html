Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get the full 180Hz refresh rate via HDMI?

No, the HDMI 2.0 ports on this monitor support up to 144Hz at QHD resolution. To achieve the maximum 180Hz refresh rate, you must use the DisplayPort 1.4 connection.

Does this monitor have built-in speakers?

No, the GS27QCA does not include built-in speakers. However, it features a dedicated 3.5mm earphone jack, allowing you to easily connect your favorite gaming headset or external speakers directly to the monitor.
